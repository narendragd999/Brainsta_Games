How to Play Classic Tetris IQ Challenge

Use arrow keys or buttons/swipe on mobile:

  • Left/Right (←/→): Move piece
  • Down (↓): Soft drop
  • Rotate (↻): Rotate piece
  • Hard Drop (⇊): Drop instantly
  • P: Pause
  • R: Restart
