How to Play Kakuro

Kakuro is a puzzle game often referred to as a mathematical crossword.

Rules:

  • Fill the grid with digits from 1 to 9
  • The sum of digits in each "word" must equal the clue shown in the shaded box
  • No digit can be repeated within the same "word"

Clue Boxes:

Shaded boxes contain clues. The number on the top-right is the sum for the vertical word below, and the number on the bottom-left is the sum for the horizontal word to the right.

How to Play:

  • Click on a white cell to select it
  • Use the number pad to enter a value
  • Click "Check Solution" to verify your answers
  • Click "New Game" to start a different puzzle
